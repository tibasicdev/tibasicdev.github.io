# TI-Basic Humor
1. Why did the 82 fall on the annual TI calc mountain climbing contest? *It has no hooks.*
1. What do you call an error that lives at 13 Error Alley? *ERR:BAD ADDRESS*
1. What do you call an error that's uneducated and wears mismatched clothes? *ERR: DIM MISMATCH*
1. What do you call a 15-year-old error that tries to buy a beer? *ERR: ID NOT FOUND*
1. What do you call an error that illegally owns a bird? *ERR: ILLEGAL NEST*
1. What do you call an error that doesn't own two mats? *ERR: SINGULAR MAT*
1. What do you call an error that drove by at 90 mph? *ERR: ZOOM*
1. What do you call an error that tried to climb in a window and got stuck? *ERR: WINDOW RANGE*
1. What do you call an error that forgot to take off the "on sale" sign? *ERR: NO SIGN CHNG*
1. What do you call an error that forgets? *ERR: MEMORY*
1. What do you call a stupid error in a wheelchair? *ERR: INVALID DIM*
1. What do you call an error with a name tag? *ERR: LABEL*
1. What do you call any other error? *ERR: UNDEFINED*
1. Why did Pxl-On(, Pxl-Off(, and Pxl-Change( not go to the party? *They're not interactive.*
1. Why did the buyers not know how much the calculator cost? *The salesman took the LabelOff.*
1. Why do or and xor have more free time? *Because and gets evaluated first.*
1. What do you call a dead error? *ERR: EXPIRED*
1. What do you call an error that always contradicts you? *ERR: ARGUMENT*
1. What do you call an error that says you can't park here? *ERR: RESERVED*
1. What did the calculator say to the inconsiderate user? *F(n)Off!!!*
1. What did the calculator say to the drowning user? *Float!*
1. What did the calculator ask the eating user? *Full?*
1. What do you call a calculator that's always on time? *Prompt*
1. What do you call a calc that's taking out the trash? *Garbage Collecting...*
1. What's black and blue and crying? *An 83+ in lcd test mode.*
1. What's black and blue and dead? *Same 83+, an hour later.*
