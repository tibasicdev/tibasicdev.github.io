Some commands have a superscript next to them that indicates compatibility:
- <sup>83+</sup> indicates that the command requires a TI-83+, TI-83+SE, TI-84+, or TI-84+SE calculator.
- <sup>84+</sup> indicates that the command requires a TI-84+ or TI-84+SE calculator.
- <sup>84+2.30</sup> indicates that the command requires a TI-84+ or TI-84+SE calculator with OS 2.30 or higher.
