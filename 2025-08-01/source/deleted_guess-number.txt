This program identifies whether a number is prime.

NOTE: This program does not display a correct result for 0, 1, or 2, which are special cases.

PROGRAM: PRIME
:ClrHome
:Prompt X
:­1→Y
:For(P,2,√(X)+1)
:If fPart(X/P)*P=0
:Then
:P→Y
:Goto E
:End
:End
:Lbl E
:If Y≠­1
:Then
:Disp X
:Disp "Composite"
:Disp "Divisible by"
:Disp Y
:End
:If Y=­1
:Then
:Disp X
:Disp "Is prime"
:End
