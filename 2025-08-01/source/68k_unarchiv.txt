The {{Unarchiv}} command removes a variable from the flash ROM.
[[code]]
Prgm
©                                //assuming var1 and var2 are actual variables
Archive var1,var2
isArchiv(var1)→check
Disp check
Pause
Unarchive var1,var2
Disphome
EndPrgm
[[/code]]
