This is used to clear the IO buffer (not the home screen). The IO buffer is where any {{Output()}}, {{Disp}}, {{Pause}}, and any other commands that use the I/O buffer display results.
[[code]]
:ClrIO
:Disp "Hello"
[[/code]]
