Bomber v1.0 is an arcade game. The goal is to defuse as many bombs as you can. To defuse a bomb, you press a key from F1 to F5 corresponding to the bomb you want to defuse. When you do defuse a bomb, the counter will start back. To defuse a bomb successfully, you must stop the counter between two given numbers accordingly to the difficulty you're playing.

Features an highscore system, three difficulties, entirely on graphscreen, Clear to quit whenever you want, in-game instructions and no installation.
