|location=From the program editor, press [F3][2]
|size=X byte(s)
]]
The Disp command displays output in the Home App I/O screen. An example:
[[code]]
prgmexmp()
Prgm
Request "Enter something",var1   //Get a value for var1
Disp "var1=",var1
Pause
DelVar var1
ClrIO
DispHome
EndPrgm
[[/code]]
