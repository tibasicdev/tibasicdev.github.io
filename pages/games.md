# Games
The programs linked from this page are complete (if simple) games, presented with source code and an explanation. They are meant as a way of putting everything that is on this guide together into an example of what TI-Basic programmers like you can do. For readers who learn better by example than by explanation, these games are also a great way to learn the techniques explained in this guide.

------

- **[Pong](pong.html)** — You have to keep the ball in play by bouncing it back and forth against the walls, without letting it get by your paddle.
- **[Mine Field](mine-field.html)** — Like the classic game minesweeper, the goal is to avoid hitting the mines.
- **[Guess The Number](guess-the-number.html)** — Try to guess the number, and see how many tries it takes you.
- **[Darts](darts.html)** — Hit the target with your dart, while avoiding the obstacles in your way.
- **[Tic-Tac-To](tic-tac-to.html)** — Alternate turns placing pieces on the 3x3 game board until somebody gets 3-in-a-row or all nine spots on the game board are filled.
- **[Mastermind](mastermind.html)** (*[alternative](mastermind2.html)*) — Try to break a random code of five numbers in fifteen guesses or less.
- **[Avalanche](avalanche.html)** — Avoid the falling spikes by moving to the left or right, trying to see how long you can hold out for.
- **[Snake](snake.html)** — Go around eating food, and the snake grows longer and longer with each piece of food.
- **[Memory](memory.html)** — A simple "card" game where you flip over two cards each turn to try to get a matching pair.
- **[Hangman](hangman.html)** — Alternate turns trying to guess a word by selecting the letters that make up the word until the word is correctly guessed or you run out of chances.
- **[Rock Paper Scissors](rock-paper-scissors.html)** — You have three different hand gestures to choose from, but can you choose the best hand...
- **[Demented Snake](demented-snake.html)** — Just like snake, except you need to constantly change directions to keep the snake moving fast.
- **[Hop Over](hop-over.html)** — You have to move the pieces around until you get the four squares on the left and the four cross-hairs on the right.
- **[Fly The Copter](fly-the-copter.html)** — You have to navigate a scrolling tunnel for as long as possible.
- **[Simon Says](simon-says.html)** — You must repeat a pattern which gets longer and longer.
- **[Maze Walkthrough](maze-walkthrough.html)** — You have to make your way through the maze without touching any of the walls.
- **[Chase](chase.html)** — You have to chase down the randomly moving target.
- **[Light Cycles](light-cycles.html)** — Control your continuous cycle and out last the AI.
- **[Connect 4, 2 player](connect-4-2-player.html)** — take turns dropping checkers into a grid to make 4 in a row Horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
- **[Mancala](mancala.html)** — drop seeds or stones into houses to either clear your side first or to get the most stones in your end house, depending on your rule set.
